Pancakes

prep-time 5min | cook-time 10min | serves 2

You will need:
  • 1 cup of oats
  • 1/2 cup of mil (your choise, but tastes fantastic with oat milk)
  • 1 ripe banana
  • pinch of salt (small pinch)
  • tsp vanilla essence (optional)
  • tbsp maple syrup (optional)
  • youghurt of your choice
  • berries and fruit of your choice
  1. Put all of the ingredients into a blender. Allow your blender to combine the engredients into a smooth batter.
  2. Place a spoonfull of batter into a pre-heated and lightly oiled frying pan.
  3. Flip the pancake after couple minutes once it's browned. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Serve with yoghurt, berries, jam, nutella ( or anything that tickles your fancy).
